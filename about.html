About PokeType

Ideas that brought the game to life and Creator behind PokeType and the passion.

Rushing Pokémon towards you

  Rushing Pokémon charge toward you from every direction, testing your speed, focus, and typing skills under pressure.

Type quickly

  Type quickly and accurately to catch each Pokémon before it reaches you, proving your speed, precision, and focus in every battle.

Survive each wave and advance

  Survive each wave of rushing Pokémon and advance through the levels as the challenge becomes faster and more intense.
I’m a passionate developer who enjoys building interactive and creative web experiences. PokeType was created as a fun project combining my love for Pokémon and programming, focusing on speed, logic, and user interaction. I’m always exploring new ideas and improving my skills to build better and more exciting applications.
